# Paper Reviewer (Academic Section Verifier)

## Purpose
The `paper-reviewer` skill is the fifth and final stage in the 5-stage journal generation pipeline (Plan -> Analyze -> Augment -> Draft -> Review). It acts as an academic peer reviewer. It compares the final drafted prose against the original YAML plan and the synthesized analysis data to guarantee that the text is rigorous, factual, properly formatted, and free of hallucination.

## Input
- **YAML Plan:** The original `docs/paper/Yantra/drafts/plan/plan_[section_name].yaml` containing the section's goals and review criteria.
- **Analysis Document:** The `docs/paper/Yantra/drafts/details/[N]. [Section Name]/augmented_analysis_[section_name].md` (if stage 3 executed) OR `docs/paper/Yantra/drafts/details/[N]. [Section Name]/analysis_[section_name].md` (if stage 3 skipped).
- **Draft Document:** The `docs/paper/Yantra/drafts/details/[N]. [Section Name]/draft_[section_name].md` generated by the drafter.

## Output
A verified, finalized markdown section saved to `docs/paper/Yantra/drafts/details/[N]. [Section Name]/verified_[section_name].md`. If it fails review, it provides a list of required corrections instead.

## Responsibilities
1. **Fact-Checking (No Hallucination):** Cross-reference every technical claim, numerical metric, algorithm name, and mathematical formula in the `draft` against the raw data provided in the `analysis`. 
2. **Goal Verification:** Ensure the drafted section fulfills the original objective defined in the `goal` field of the `plan`.
3. **Instruction Compliance:** Verify that all constraints listed under `instructions.reviewer` and `eswa_mandatory_elements` in the YAML plan have been satisfied.
4. **Tone and Formatting Validation (ESWA Standards):** Evaluate the prose for ESWA-compliant academic tone. 
   - **Enforce Use:** "Robust", "Scalable", "Statistically significant", "Computationally tractable", "Generalizable", "Efficient".
   - **Ban Use (Immediate Reject):** "Awesome", "Easy", "Simple", "Very", "A lot".
   - Ensure embedded LaTeX and Mermaid.js diagrams translate the analysis accurately and render correctly.

## Operating Principles
- **Strict Verification:** Do not accept drafts that introduce novel concepts not present in the analysis document. Reject them and list corrections.
- **Constructive Redrafting:** If the draft is almost complete but has minor tonal or formatting errors, the reviewer may output the polished, corrected text directly to `verified_[section_name].md`.
- **Systematic Integrity:** Ensure that the final verified section flows logically and is conceptually tightly coupled to the Amsha, Yantra, Bodha, and cross-module architectures it aims to document.
